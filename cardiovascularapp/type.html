Types of Heart Disease

  1. Coronary Heart Disease
    • Atheroclerosis happens when plaque accumulates on the arterial walls. This leads to them becoming rigid and narrow, restricting the flow of blood
    • Angina Rectoris: Severe chest pains
    • Myocardial Infarction: Heart attack when one or more of the heart arteries are blocked
    • Arrhythmia: Non-standard heart rhythm which leads to cardiac arrest
  2. Heart Attack
    • Lower jaw pain
    • Middle back pain
    • Left arm pain
    • Shortness of Breath
  3. Stroke
    • Blockage that occurs on the way to the brain, rupture in the arteries of the brain which is also known as a brain attack
    • Types of Strokes
      • Ischemic
      • Hemorrhagic
      • Trans-ischemic or TIA
  4. Cincinnati Stroke Scale
    • Face: Check for smile and ask the question, Is one side like the other?
    • Arm: Check for gross body defects
    • Speech: Is their speech slurred
    • Time: If one of the previous three is the case, call 911
