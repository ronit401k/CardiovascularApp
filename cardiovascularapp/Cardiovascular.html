Cardiovascular Disease

What is Cardivascular Disease ?

  1. Diseases related to the heart such as heart attacks, strokes, valve problems and the most prevalent, Coronary Art Disease
  2. Cardiovascular disease is also the leading cause of deaths worldwide, particularly in America
  3. Symptoms
    • Chest pain or discomfort
    • Irregular Heartbeats aka arrhythmias
  4. Main Signs
    • Heart Attack
    • Stroke
