Cardiovascular Disease

How to Prevent Heart Disease?

  1. Monitor levels and intake of cholesterol, specifically cholesterol high in LDL (Low-Density Lipoprotein)
  2. Check Blood Pressure
  3. Your diet should be rich in food and vegetables. However, there should be a balance between Foods, Vegetables, Whole Grains, and HDL (High-Density Lipoprotien) fats like avocados
  4. Exercise frequently
  5. Maintain a healthy weight and low BMI
  6. Limit salt intake
  7. Limit use of tobacco
  8. Regulate diabetic levels
